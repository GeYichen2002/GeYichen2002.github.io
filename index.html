"My mission is to bridge generational health gaps through media that educates, connects, and inspires action."

Through my work as a health communicator and social media influencer, I strive to address the unique health challenges facing different generations in Chinese society. By creating accessible, engaging content that speaks to cultural contexts and family dynamics, I aim to foster healthier communities and stronger support systems.

My approach combines rigorous research with compelling storytelling, allowing complex health information to reach diverse audiences in formats they can understand and apply to their lives.

About Me

Yichen Ge

Health Communication Specialist & Media Producer

I am a dedicated health media specialist with a passion for addressing intergenerational health challenges through innovative communication strategies. With expertise spanning digital storytelling, community engagement, and family-centered health initiatives, I work to bridge communication gaps and promote wellbeing across age groups in Chinese society.

My professional journey includes impactful roles at leading organizations including Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book), where I developed content strategies that increased engagement in family health communities by 24%. At People's Daily, I managed content for government health departments and led initiatives integrating traditional Chinese wellness concepts with modern health practices. My work with Blue Boat Family Therapy Studio has allowed me to translate complex psychological concepts into accessible media that strengthens family communication.

Through targeted initiatives like the "Behind the Cloud" youth vaping prevention campaign, "Beyond the Toast" workplace wellness program, and "Connected Generations" digital literacy series, I've demonstrated how culturally-sensitive media can transform health behaviors. My approach combines rigorous research with compelling storytelling, making complex health information accessible to diverse audiences.
